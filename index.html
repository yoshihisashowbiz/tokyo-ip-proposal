03 — Implementation

What happens

Guests do not just stay.
They encounter a city that never existed.

Each room becomes a different memory of Tokyo. The hotel transforms from a place to stay into a place to experience an alternative reality.

Installation

The project is implemented as a layered system:

  • • Anchor: A central work defining the space
  • • Variation: Multiple works distributed across rooms
  • • Transition: Subtle elements that appear in between

In addition, fragments appear throughout the space, sometimes in the form of small cards. Not as explanation, but as traces of the experience.

Pilot

The project can begin on a small scale:

  • • 1 floor
  • • 3–5 works
  • • Optional card-based elements

Flexible depending on space and concept.

This is not an art installation.

It is a way to redefine how a hotel is experienced.

In a city like Tokyo, where everything already exists, what remains valuable is what has never existed.

This project introduces a layer of memory that cannot be replicated elsewhere.

This aligns with hotels that position themselves as cultural platforms, not just places to stay.

Designed for a hotel that values culture, not just stay.
