01 — Concept

Two series.
One impossible city.

Every city produces residue — light that doesn't belong, surfaces that outlast their purpose, reactions that occur without witnesses. TOKYO IP extracts these moments and reconstitutes them as a coherent visual system.

This is intellectual property, not decoration. The works do not illustrate Tokyo. They generate a Tokyo that never existed — available exclusively through licensed deployment.

Series I — Ghost

Residual Tokyo

What the city leaves behind after attention has moved on. Threshold, passage, sediment, periphery — five works tracing the archaeology of an ordinary moment.

Series II — Chemical

Synthetic Tokyo

The city as reaction: severance, precipitation, saturation, cooling — five works mapping the physics of urban intensity at the moment it exceeds itself.

03 — Implementation

What happens

Guests do not just stay. They encounter a city that never existed.

Each room becomes a different memory of Tokyo. The hotel transforms from a place to stay into a place to experience an alternative reality.

Installation

The project is implemented as a layered system:

  • • Anchor: A central piece defining the space
  • • Variation: Multiple works distributed across rooms
  • • Transition: Subtle elements that appear in between

In addition, fragments appear throughout the space, sometimes in the form of small cards. Not as explanation, but as residual matter of the experience.

Pilot Scale
  • • 1 floor / 3–5 works
  • • Optional card system

Flexible depending on space and concept.
