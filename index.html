01 — Concept

Two series.
One impossible city.

Every city produces residue — light that doesn't belong, surfaces that outlast their purpose, reactions that occur without witnesses. TOKYO IP extracts these moments and reconstitutes them as a coherent visual system.

This is intellectual property, not decoration. The works do not illustrate Tokyo. They generate a Tokyo that never existed — available exclusively through licensed deployment.

Series I — Ghost

Residual Tokyo

What the city leaves behind after attention has moved on. Threshold, passage, sediment, periphery — five works tracing the archaeology of an ordinary moment.

Series II — Chemical

Synthetic Tokyo

The city as reaction: severance, precipitation, saturation, cooling — five works mapping the physics of urban intensity at the moment it exceeds itself.
